Overview

Purpose

My purpose is to provide a place to keep all of the alternate posters for my favorirte films.

Audience

The Audience is going to be myself. This site will serve to be a place I can go back to whenever I want to use a poster when discussing a movie online or for photoshop. It puts a personalized archive in a single place for me to use effisciently.
