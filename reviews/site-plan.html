Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to show a few of my proudest Letterboxd reviews in a more easily sharable format. It will feature a few selections from a social media site, including roughly 3 to 4 reviews that I think highlight fresh new insights and add something to the conversation of film analysis.

Audience

My audience is many. My parents don't really understand a lot of technology, so if I show them this site, then they'll see what I have to say in a more effective way. Another audience is possible employers, as this site can serve as a portfolio incease I choose to work in the movie reveiw industry.
